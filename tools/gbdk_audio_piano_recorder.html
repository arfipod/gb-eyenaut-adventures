How to integrate

  1. Download the generated .h.
  2. Include it from your GBDK project.
  3. Call gb_audio_init(); once.
  4. Call eyenaut_theme_update(); once per frame.
  5. Call eyenaut_theme_start(); to play.

GB sound register model

Square channels use a Game Boy frequency register value computed as:
gb_freq = 2048 - 131072 / hz

Playback stores note index, duration in frames, duty, volume and channel. For serious music, migrate later to hUGEDriver; for SFX and short themes, this is enough.
